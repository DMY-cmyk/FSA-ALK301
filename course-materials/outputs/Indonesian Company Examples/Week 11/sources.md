### Notes
- All financial data sourced from publicly available audited consolidated financial statements filed with Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) and OJK.
- BUMI reports in US Dollars (USD) as its functional currency due to predominantly USD-denominated revenues and costs.
- The periods of negative equity (2019–2021) reflect cumulative losses exceeding paid-in capital and reserves; this constitutes technical insolvency.
- Market capitalization estimates for Z-score X₄ component use approximate share prices from IDX historical data multiplied by shares outstanding as reported in financial statements.
- Peer data (ADRO, ITMG) from respective companies' audited financial statements for FY2022, filed with IDX.
- Altman Z-score calculations use the original 1968 model formula for publicly traded manufacturing/resource companies.
- Debt restructuring details compiled from BUMI annual reports, IDX disclosures, and publicly available court filings.
