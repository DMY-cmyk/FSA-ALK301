### Regulatory Framework Sources
- **OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan)**: https://www.ojk.go.id/
  - POJK on bank capital adequacy requirements (Basel III implementation)
  - Minimum CAR requirements including capital conservation buffer, countercyclical buffer, D-SIB surcharge
- **Bank Indonesia**: https://www.bi.go.id/
  - Macroprudential regulations
- **LPS (Lembaga Penjamin Simpanan)**: https://www.lps.go.id/
  - Deposit insurance coverage (currently Rp 2 billion per depositor per bank)
- **Basel Committee on Banking Supervision**: https://www.bis.org/bcbs/
  - Basel III framework — the international standard adopted by OJK

### Notes
- Financial data from BBCA 2022-2023 is used to illustrate financing activities and capital structure concepts.
- All ratios are approximate and computed from publicly available audited financial statements filed with IDX.
- BBCA data is used to demonstrate why conventional leverage ratios and the original Z-score model are not directly applicable to banks.
- ASII data is included for cross-sector comparison to highlight industry-specific differences in capital structure.
- Capital adequacy ratios are taken from BBCA's regulatory filings and Notes to Financial Statements.
- Market capitalization and credit ratings are as of December 2023.
- Cost of funds estimate is approximated from management discussion in the Annual Report.
