### Notes
- All financial data sourced from publicly available audited consolidated financial statements.
- HMSP uses PSAK (Indonesian adoption of IFRS).
- PMI owns approximately 92.5% of HMSP shares.
- Revenue figures are **net of excise taxes** as reported in HMSP's income statement.
- Dividend payout ratios exceeding 100% are funded from accumulated retained earnings / cash reserves.
- Valuation multiples based on market prices as of late 2023 / early 2024.
- GGRM comparison data from audited financial statements FY2023.
- All ratios computed by the author from raw financial statement data.
- Cash flow figures rounded to one decimal place (trillions of Rupiah).
- DuPont decomposition uses average equity and average total assets for turnover calculations.
- Prospective analysis scenarios represent author estimates based on historical trends and announced excise policy.
