# Week 14 — Indonesian Company Example Sources

## IDX Delisted Companies (Various)
### Focus: Bankruptcy Prediction — Applying Z-Score & O-Score Concepts to Indonesian Corporate Failures (BTEL, BLTA, DAVO, TRUB)

### Primary Sources — IDX Regulatory Framework
- **IDX Listing Rules (Peraturan Pencatatan BEI)**: Bursa Efek Indonesia; https://www.idx.co.id/id/peraturan/peraturan-pencatatan/
- **IDX Fact Book (annual)**: Annual statistical publication of IDX; https://www.idx.co.id/id/data-pasar/laporan-statistik/fact-book/
- **IDX Delisting Announcements**: Official announcements published via IDX website and IDXChannel; https://www.idx.co.id/
- **OJK Regulations on Delisting**: Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (Financial Services Authority); https://www.ojk.go.id/

### Primary Sources — Indonesian Bankruptcy Law
- **UU No. 37 Tahun 2004**: Undang-Undang tentang Kepailitan dan Penundaan Kewajiban Pembayaran Utang (PKPU); accessible via https://peraturan.bpk.go.id/
- **Pengadilan Niaga (Commercial Court) Decisions**: Published case decisions; accessible via https://putusan3.mahkamahagung.go.id/

### Primary Sources — BTEL (PT Bakrie Telecom Tbk)
- **Annual Reports 2008–2014**: PT Bakrie Telecom Tbk; filed with IDX prior to suspension
- **IDX Suspension & Delisting Notices**: BEI announcement regarding BTEL suspension (2014) and forced delisting (2020)
- **PKPU Filing Documentation**: Pengadilan Niaga Jakarta Pusat; homologation of debt restructuring plan (2014)
- **IDX Company Profile (historical)**: https://www.idx.co.id/ — BTEL company page (archived)

### Primary Sources — BLTA (PT Berlian Laju Tanker Tbk)
- **Annual Reports 2006–2012**: PT Berlian Laju Tanker Tbk; filed with IDX prior to suspension
- **IDX Suspension & Delisting Notices**: BEI announcement regarding BLTA suspension and forced delisting (2018)
- **PKPU Filing Documentation**: Pengadilan Niaga Jakarta Pusat; PKPU proceedings (2012)

### Primary Sources — DAVO (PT Davomas Abadi Tbk)

### Notes
- Financial data for delisted companies is limited post-delisting; pre-delisting annual reports filed with IDX are the primary data source.
- BTEL, BLTA, and DAVO data points are approximate based on publicly available reports prior to suspension; exact figures may vary across sources.
- TRUB (now TBS Energi Utama/TOBA) was restructured rather than permanently delisted — included as a contrast case showing potential recovery.
- Z-score and O-score variables presented in the analysis are conceptual applications based on directional patterns rather than precise calculations, due to limited availability of granular financial data for delisted companies.
- Indonesian bankruptcy/PKPU process timelines differ significantly from US Chapter 7/11 proceedings; direct comparison of "years to failure" with Altman/Deakin/Ohlson samples requires caution.
- Currency effects (IDR/USD) on debt ratios for BLTA are a significant confounding factor not present in the original US-based models.
