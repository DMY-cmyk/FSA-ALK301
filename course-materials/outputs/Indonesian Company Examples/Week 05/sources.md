### Notes
- All financial data sourced from publicly available audited financial statements filed with Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX).
- ASII consolidated data includes all subsidiaries (>200 entities); UNTR data reflects UNTR's own consolidated financials.
- Ownership percentages are direct holdings by ASII unless otherwise noted; effective ownership through multi-layer chains may differ.
- Ratios are approximate, computed from rounded figures in published financial statements.
- Goodwill impairment testing is performed annually by ASII; no material impairments disclosed for FY2022-2023.
- Bank Permata ownership (~44.56%) is an equity method investment following the change in control structure with Bangkok Bank.
- Conglomerate discount analysis is based on general market observations; specific discount figures were not computed.
- The Debt-to-Equity ratio is approximate, computed as (Total Assets - Total Equity) / Total Equity as a proxy.
