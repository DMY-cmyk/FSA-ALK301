# Week 01 — Indonesian Company Example Sources

## UNVR — Unilever Indonesia Tbk

### Primary Sources
- **Annual Report 2023**: https://www.unilever.co.id/investor-relations/
- **Financial Statements 2023 (audited)**: IDX filing via https://www.idx.co.id/id/perusahaan-tercatat/laporan-keuangan-dan-tahunan/
- **Company Profile**: IDX Fact Book 2024

| Total Equity 2023 | Rp 4.4 trillion | Audited FS 2023, Balance Sheet |

### Notes
- Financial data from UNVR 2023 is used as a contemporary parallel to Colgate-Palmolive 2011 data in Subramanyam Ch.1.
