# Week 02 — Indonesian Company Example Sources

### Focus: Financial Reporting Environment, PSAK Compliance, Accounting Policy Choices

### Primary Sources
- **Annual Report 2023**: https://www.unilever.co.id/investor-relations/

- **Independent Auditor's Report 2023**: KAP Siddharta Widjaja & Rekan (member of KPMG International)
