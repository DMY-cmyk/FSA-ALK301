### Notes
- All financial data sourced from publicly available audited consolidated financial statements filed with Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) and OJK.
- BBRI uses PSAK (Indonesian adoption of IFRS). Key banking-specific standards include PSAK 71 (ECL model for provisions) and PAPI guidelines.
- ROE and ROA figures as reported by BBRI use average equity and average assets respectively. DuPont decomposition in this analysis uses end-of-period figures for simplicity, causing minor differences vs. reported ratios.
- Equity Multiplier is computed as Total Assets / Equity attributable to parent entity (excluding non-controlling interests).
- Provision Expense / Average Loans ratio (credit cost) is an annualized figure based on the provision for credit losses recognized in the income statement divided by average total loans outstanding.
- Sustainable Growth Rate (SGR) is computed as ROE × (1 − Dividend Payout Ratio). Payout ratio is DPS / EPS for the respective year.
- BBRI's high payout ratio (~80%) reflects Indonesian SOE (BUMN) dividend policy, where the government as majority shareholder requires significant dividend payments for state budget (APBN) contribution.
- Cross-sectional comparison data for BMRI and BBCA are from their respective audited financial statements and annual reports for FY2023.
- The ROE change attribution analysis (2021→2023) is an approximation computed by isolating the marginal impact of each DuPont component while holding other components constant. Due to interaction effects, individual attributions may not sum precisely to the total change.
- Loan-at-Risk (LAR) ratio includes performing loans under COVID restructuring programs (POJK 11/2020) — this metric is specific to the Indonesian regulatory context and not directly comparable with international standards.
- All computed ratios use rounded figures from published financial statements and may contain minor rounding differences.
