### Notes
- All financial data sourced from publicly available audited consolidated financial statements filed with Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) and OJK.
- GOTO uses PSAK (Indonesian adoption of IFRS).
- GOTO completed the Gojek-Tokopedia merger in 2021 and IPO'd in April 2022. Pre-merger financials are combined/carve-out figures and may not be directly comparable to post-merger consolidated statements.
- The massive net loss in 2022 (Rp 40.4T) includes a non-cash goodwill impairment of Rp 24.6T. Adjusted for this, the operating loss was approximately Rp 15.8T.
- Adjusted EBITDA is a non-GAAP metric reported by GOTO management that excludes stock-based compensation, goodwill impairment, and certain non-recurring items.
- Cash runway calculation is a simplified estimate assuming constant burn rate. Actual runway depends on revenue trajectory, cost reduction progress, and potential additional financing.
- TLKM comparison data from PT Telkom Indonesia (Persero) Tbk audited financial statements FY2023, used as a benchmark for mature Indonesian company with stable, predictable financial performance.
- Gross margin improvement from -161% (2021) to 20.2% (2023) reflects GOTO's transition from heavy subsidization of transactions to more sustainable unit economics.
