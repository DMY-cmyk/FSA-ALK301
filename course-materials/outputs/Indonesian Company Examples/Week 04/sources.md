### Segment Information Sources
- ASII segments (per annual report): Automotive, Financial Services, Heavy Equipment Mining Construction & Energy (HEMCE), Agribusiness, Infrastructure & Logistics, Information Technology, Property
- Segment assets and capital expenditure are disclosed in the Notes to Financial Statements under "Segment Reporting" (PSAK 5 / IFRS 8)
- HEMCE segment is primarily United Tractors (UNTR) and Pamapersada Nusantara
- Agribusiness segment is primarily Astra Agro Lestari (AALI)

### Subsidiary Annual Reports (supplementary)
- **United Tractors (UNTR)**: https://www.unitedtractors.com/investor-relations/ — for mining properties and heavy equipment PP&E detail
- **Astra Agro Lestari (AALI)**: https://www.astra-agro.co.id/investor-relations/ — for bearer plants and plantation assets detail

### Accounting Standards Referenced
- **PSAK 16** (IAS 16) — Property, Plant and Equipment: cost model, revaluation model, depreciation
- **PSAK 19** (IAS 38) — Intangible Assets: recognition, amortization, development cost capitalization
- **PSAK 48** (IAS 36) — Impairment of Assets: recoverable amount, CGU, goodwill impairment testing
- **PSAK 69** (IAS 41) — Agriculture: bearer plants reclassified as PP&E
- **PSAK 13** (IAS 40) — Investment Property: fair value model vs cost model
- **PSAK 64** (IFRS 6) — Exploration for and Evaluation of Mineral Resources

### Notes
- Financial data from ASII 2021-2023 is used to illustrate investing activities and asset quality concepts.
- All ratios are approximate and computed from publicly available audited financial statements filed with IDX.
- The large capex figure for 2023 (Rp 45.9 trillion) includes both organic PP&E additions and strategic investments/acquisitions across segments.
- PP&E net book value increased significantly from 2022 to 2023, partly due to reclassification and new consolidations.
- Mining properties are classified separately from PP&E in ASII's balance sheet but are analyzed together for asset quality purposes.
- Bearer plants (PSAK 69/IAS 41) are reclassified as PP&E for mature biological assets.
- Goodwill impairment testing is performed annually; no material impairments were recognized in 2021-2023.
- Market data and segment information are as reported in annual reports and press releases.
